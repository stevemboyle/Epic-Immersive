Mélanie Dorey

Creative Director, Madame Lupin | Paris, France

Underland Artist-in Residence, Epic Immersive

Biography

Melanie Dorey

Mélanie Dorey is the Founder and Creative Director of Paris' Madame Lupin.

Madame Lupin has created luscious, multidisciplinary experiences that range from art party experiences in the Paris catacombs to scent-based immersive experiences inspired by Alice in Wonderland.

Mélanie teamed up with Epic Immersive to create the Paris Immersive Creation Challenge, an international hackathon which brought 40 leading global artists to Paris to create a new immersive experience in less than 48 hours.

During her Underland residency with Epic Immersive, Mélanie and her collaborator Pim Schachtschabel of Amsterdam's Sherlocked crafted a new Underland version of their experience Conversation Restaurant, which had been originally workshopped at Stone Soup and The College of Extraordinary Experiences.

Mélanie is a pioneer of the Parisian immersive scene and a community organizer. She is the founder of the blog Théâtre Immersif and the French correspondent for No Proscenium.
