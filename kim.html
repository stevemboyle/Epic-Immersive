Biography

Kimberly Flynn

Kimberly Flynn joined Epic for 2017's sellout hit The Infernal Motel, where she played the played two tracks: Virgil/Francesca and Helen of Troy. From there, Kim created the role of a monk in The Queen on the Mountain, an immersive experience designed for Google at The Mountain Winery, and played Tweedle-Dum in Wonderland, an underground experience designed for Facebook based on Alice in Wonderland.

Outside of Epic Immersive, Kim was a member of the San Francisco cast of the immersive ASMR experience Whisperlodge (featured in Buzzfeed, the Atlantic, and The New York Times).
