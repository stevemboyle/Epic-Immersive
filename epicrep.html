New Stories in Wonderland & Neverland

Join Us on Three Adventures

Wonderland and Neverland are too big for just one story. Slide down into the worlds of your dreams, and find yourself at the center of the action...

This September and October, Epic is rolling out three immersive experiences in repertory. Get ready to enter the worlds of...

The Changeling: A Neverland Story

Get ready to embark on a brand-new adventure through Neverland with The Changeling: A Neverland Story. In this all-new, action-packed two-hour experience, you'll plunge into an island filled with fairie intrigue and a pirate quests. Part Treasure Island, part A Midsummer Night's Dream, and all Neverland -- The Changeling is an action-packed adventure, with a two-hour-long, cinematic plotline that will place you at the center of the action in this origin story for our Neverland series.

Carnival of Memories: A Wonderland Story

In Carnival of Memories: A Wonderland Story, join Alice and her sister Margaret on a new brand-new, two-hour quest through a Festival in Wonderland in search of the secrets of Alice's lineage. You'll navigate games, shows, a hunt for the Jabberwocky, and a rabbit hole that takes you back in time to Wonderland's past as you unravel the mysteries of Alice's childhood and her fantasy land.

Between Two Worlds: A Wonderland Story

Between Two Worlds: A Wonderland Story is a revamped edition of our original Wonderland, with new characters and new environments. If you've been before, there are new scenes to encounter. Make sure to snag a red-suited ticket to meet all-new characters!

Carnival of Memories:

A Wonderland Story

A New Adventure Down the Rabbit Hole

Tickets on Sale September 12th!


Wonderland goes deeper than you ever imagined. The Rabbit Hole is waiting...

It's time to return to Wonderland! This time, you'll get swept up in a Wonderland-wide festival, full of games, shows, and a hunt for the Jabberwocky. Prepare to step into Wonderland's past, and unearth the mystery of Alice's lineage... Along the way, you'll enter the Caterpillar's hookah den, play croquet with the Tweedles, get to know the Red and White Queens, join a troupe of actors, and burrow through rabbit holes within rabbit holes until you get a taste of Wonderland back in the day!

The Story of Carnival of Memories: A Wonderland Story

Alice is all grown up, and she's expecting! Her sister Margaret is hosting a baby shower for Alice and her friends (that's us), though the day is somewhat overshadowed by the recent passing of their mother, the stern Lorina. When Margaret discovers notes suggesting that Lorina had visited Wonderland as a child, Alice and Margaret will plunge down the rabbit hole on a mission to retrace their mother's footsteps that will find them in the midst of a rollicking festival, a royal jubilee, a hunt for the Jabberwocky, and a journey through time.

Tickets and Chronology

Carnival of Memories is a standalone sequel to our original Wonderland, now titled Between Two Worlds. You absolutely need no prior knowledge to enjoy Carnival of Memories, but your experience of Alice will be deepened by seeing both! Carnival of Memories is a two-hour long experience. Tickets are $85.

The Changeling:

A Neverland Story

Embark on an All-New Island Adventure

Tickets on Sale September 12th!


Be at the center of your own adventure story...

Enter Neverland, a world of mystery of danger, filled with fairies, pirates, mermaids, and sprites. Get ready to stowaway on pirate ships, rescue lost boys, swim with mermaids, meet the Boy King, and win the favor of fairies as you get roped into a daunting pirate adventure and intrigue within the fairie court. Part Treasure Island, part A Midsummer Night's Dream, and all Neverland -- The Changeling is an action-packed adventure, with a two-hour-long, cinematic plotline that will place you at the center of the action.

The Story of The Changeling: A Neverland Story

On the eve of our friend Madison's 21st birthday, her sister Haley falls into a mysterious fantasy world, leading us to follow her to the Never Never Land and setting into motion three quests. Rival pirates Blackbead and Long John Silver pursue a map to the Fountain of Youth as the Faerie Midwife Mab works to overthrow the Faerie Queen Titania. Meanwhile, the Eternal Boy Peter Pan seeks to rescue his favorite Lost Boy, James, from the clutches of Blackbeard, who longs to raise James as his boatswain. When all three journeys converge, we find ourselves at the Waters of Eternity, unveiling a secret to Neverland that will forever change life on the island.

Tickets and Chronology

The Changeling is a brand-new experience, and the first in our Neverland chronology. If you saw our original Neverland this summer, then you can think of The Changeling as an origin story and a prequel. If not, then get ready to be introduced to the island anew! The Changeling is a two-hour experience, and tickets are $85.

Between Two Worlds:

A Wonderland Story

Dive Down the Rabbit Hole

Tickets go on sale September 12!


Alice had her turn. Now it's yours.

Wonderland awaits... Get ready to dive down the Rabbit Hole. Between Two Worlds is an enhanced version of our original hit Wonderland, featuring brand-new environments and a few all-new scenes and characters. If you think you've seen Wonderland, think again... There's so much more to explore!

If you've already seen Wonderland, get ready to experience a few new scenes, as each of our three waves has new bonus encounters. Or, make sure that you choose a red-suited ticket for a few all-new characters.

The Story of Between Two Worlds: A Wonderland Story

It's the day of Alice's wedding, and she's a bit nervous about growing up and leaving her childhood behind. Oh well, that makes it the perfect moment for one last dip into Wonderland, where Alice will find herself torn between her past and her future. Alice's wedding party (that's us!) dive down the rabbit hole after her, leading us on an adventure where we'll encounter the Cheshire Cat, have tea with the Mad Hatter, and flee the Red Queen -- if we hope to keep our heads.

Tickets and Chronology

Between Two Worlds is the first experience in our Wonderland series. Between Two worlds is a 65-minute long experience. Tickets are $65.

About Epic Immersive

Epic Immersive designs large-scale live immersive theatre and mixed reality experiences where story, spectacle, and interaction deliver transcendent, awe-inspiring journeys.

Epic Immersive builds inspiring fictional worlds, from the 14-acre, 100-actor fictional town of Matthew Briar and the Age of Resurrection to the underworld playground of The Infernal Motel, which sold out its entire six-month run.

Epic's live experiences have been commissioned by Silicon Valley companies like Apple, Google, and Facebook. Epic was also the lead producer for The Immersive Design Summit, a first-of-its-kind gathering of the immersive universe featuring luminaries from Disney, Lucasfilm, ILMxLab, Punchdrunk, The Void, Meow Wolf, Two Bit Circus, and more.
