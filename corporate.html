You Are Epic.

At Epic Immersive, we're all about building innovative and unforgettable experiences that inspire and bring people together.

That's why companies like Google, Apple, and Facebook have come to Epic to have us design innovative events and experiences. From 2,000 person holiday parties to intimate team-building events, Epic can create mind-blowing experiences for you and your company.

We'll work with you to design a custom experience just for your company, where we bring to life an interactive universe you can play in. We've created parties with gambling, fortune telling, live music, circus and magic acts, interactive puzzles, walkaround characters, and immersive environments. We've built speakeasies and World's Fairs and explored underground secret societies. We've crafted team-building events with interactive characters and puzzles. Imagine uncovering clues in a phone hidden in a whiskey crate, or unlocking secret drinks in a conversation with a flapper. Imagine working with your team to solve mysteries together in a Virtual Reality universe where you can see and touch the seemingly virtual objects in the room with you.

By making our experiences custom-designed, we can create an event directly inspired by your hopes, dreams, and parameters. That way, we can be flexible in matching the feel of the whole evening, the amount of entertainment, and the price to what you're looking for.
