A New Immersive Club & Incubator

with a mission to change the face of the industry


Epic Immersive is announcing the creation of a new immersive club and incubator: a new home for experimental, experiential creations in the San Francisco Bay with a focus on elevating artists from underrepresented backgrounds and changing the face of the industry.

We're giving immersive space grants to women, queer artists, and artists color -- giving artists without adequate representation the space to explore, develop new work, and tell stories that are too often unheard.

Club members pay $25/month and get exclusive access to new experiences and experiments at below market rates ($15-$55).

Just to show we mean business:

+ March begins with a residency by Melinda Lauw of Whisperlodge, who is creating a new, hourlong, 1-on-1 ASMR experience with Epic called WHISPERS UNDERGROUND. Originally from Singapore and now a resident of San Francisco, Melinda is one of the world's leading live ASMR experience creators, and has been featured in The New York Times, BuzzFeed, The Atlantic, Refinery 29, Netflix, and more. WHISPERS UNDERGROUND will be available exclusively to members for $45 (Whisperlodge's Los Angeles show is currently selling for $150).

We'll follow that up with:

+ A new collaboration by Melanie Dorey, the Paris-based creator of Madame Lupin, in association with Pim Schachtschabel, an Amsterdam-based escape room designer from the world-renowned Sherlocked.

+ A new experiment by Epic Immersive in collaboration with Paris' Big Drama and Madame Lupin, based on a jam we are creating during Immersive Design Week.

+ A new choreographic collaboration by Emily Beloate and Maya Singer, two up-and-coming immersive directors.

Expect the coming months to be filled with residencies for local artists, a curated series of performances featuring international artists, and new Epic collaborations with everything from hip-hop artists to Indian mythology.

Plus, special club nights, drag performances, and upcoming perks for members. In month two, expect experiments delving into pervasive characters at club nights...

And that's the scheme: Members pay $25 to get access to an exclusive slate of new immersive experiences. This in turn allows Epic to play host to an onslaught of artists of color, women, nonbinary creators, and directors from other underrepresented backgrounds.

Plus, special club nights, drag performances, and upcoming perks for members. In month two, expect experiments delving into pervasive characters at club nights...

To join, enter your name here and our Membership Concierge will be in touch to get you started!
