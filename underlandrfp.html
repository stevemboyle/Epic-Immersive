The Underland RFP

Underland is a new immersive club, incubator, and secret laboratory by Epic Immersive. We are seeking residents from underrepresented backgrounds interested in showcasing and workshopping immersive experiences. These can range from immersive theatre to immersive games to performance art to puzzle/escape experiences. In fact, that's a big part of why we are doing this: to curate a hugely diverse range of work by a hugely diverse range of artists. Our focus is on identifying exciting artists who are queer, femme, trans, artists of color, deaf, or otherwise underrepresented in the arts and entertainment industries and giving them a space and an audience in which to develop and share work. Our secret laboratory is a dynamic space -- one which we can only share details about after you sign an NDA -- but we think you'll be hugely inspired and excited to create experiences there.

Right now, the way Underland residencies work is simple: thanks to Underland membership dues, we are able to give you the space for free. The budgets for projects are then entirely based on ticket sales; we'll work with you to figure out the right budget schema for your project. Projects are available to members and their guests. Some exceptions apply to make sure that residents get to properly share their work.

We are interested in all types of work and all types of artists, with a special affinity for work that makes us think, "wow, we haven't seen that before!" or "wow, we haven't seen those two forms or cultures or ideas combined before."

We have a few questions for you to fill out to get us started. You do not need to worry about your answers being either formal or comprehensive; this is a way to get the conversation started! We know the details will come from our conversations together, so first we want to get to know you! We'll ask about your background and pics of your work. You don't necessarily need a background in immersive art, or even a project idea just yet. We're interested in your interest!

Thanks! Let's get immersive!
