Epic Artist Dana Soliman in The Infernal Motel


The Monster at the Heart of the Maze

Underland is a new immersive club, incubator, and secret laboratory by Epic Immersive. We are seeking residents from underrepresented backgrounds interested in showcasing and workshopping immersive experiences. These can range from immersive theatre to immersive games to performance art to puzzle/escape experiences. In fact, that's a big part of why we are doing this: to curate a hugely diverse range of work by a hugely diverse range of artists. Our focus is on identifying exciting artists who are queer, femme, trans, artists of color, deaf, or otherwise underrepresented in the arts and entertainment industries and giving them a space and an audience in which to develop and share work. Our secret laboratory is a dynamic space -- one which we can only share details about after you sign an NDA -- but we think you'll be hugely inspired and excited to create experiences there.
