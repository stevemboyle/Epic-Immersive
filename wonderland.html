Wonderland

A New Adventure Down the Rabbit Hole

Limited Engagement: Just Eight Days


Alice had her turn. Now it's yours.

Through Alice's fireplace is the entrance to a secret world of magic, wonder, and danger -- full of new friends and strange enemies. Three guests at a time plunge down the rabbit hole for the trip of a lifetime.

Wonderland lasts for sixty minutes, with nine tickets available every hour. Note: Wonderland features smoke and haze effects as well as moments of crawling and darkness and is not recommended for guests with claustrophobia or mobility issues.

Eight Days Only:

February 27th & 28th, 2018

March 10th & 11th, 2018

March 17th & 18th, 2018

March 24th & 25th, 2018
