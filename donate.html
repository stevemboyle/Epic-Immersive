Epic Immersive's EpicLand

Two New Worlds on Market Street

Enter Neverland & Wonderland This Summer


Mind-Blowing Immersive Experiences

Epic Immersive creates mind-blowing immersive experiences. We've built 14-acre fictional cities with over a hundred actors and explorations through underground wonderlands that guests enter by plunging down a slide in a fireplace. We've crafted parties and experiences for Silicon Valley companies like Google, Facebook, and Apple, and we are the lead producer on the world's foremost gathering for the immersive industry, The Immersive Design Summit. Our experiences often sell out a month of tickets in four hours.

Now, we've been given a new space to work with, right on Market Street and Civic Center in San Francisco. Two floors, ready for two new immersive experiences: Neverland, and a new chapter of Wonderland. The up-front costs to building these two new environments is significant, so this time, we're turning to you for help to create the two immersive worlds. Will you join us in bringing these dream worlds to life?

Epic Immersive Labs is a 501C3, so donations are tax-deductible!
